Nasi Goreng of Disaster Recipe

This recipe is inspired by me the king of cooking desister Arfith for anything i cook, taste absolutely horrendous, and look completely unappetizing, even uncle roger would need ER after forced to taste my cooking.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • 200gr of leftover rice
  • 4 piece of garlic cloves
  • 2 piece of shallots
  • half onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoon of sweet soy sauce
  • 3 teaspoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of chilisauce(prefer del monte and don't use ketchup too sour)
  • half teaspoon of msg

Instructions

  1. Cook the eggs into scramble egg with a tablespoon of butter and set aside
  2. Melt the other half the butter and cook the garlics, shallots, and onion till fragrance
  3. Put in the rice then cook with max heat your stove can handle and stir until the all the aromatic coat all the rice grain
  4. Put the fire on low heat then add in the eggs and pour all the sauce and msg then stir it well until all the ingridients mixed
  5. Turn off the fire and serve the fried rice, and voila done!
